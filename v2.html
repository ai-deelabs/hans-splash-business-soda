Our story

A soda the size of the ocean

Han grew up where the surf met the sand, where every morning began with the crash and pull of the tide. The drinks on the shelf felt flat by comparison — too sweet, too still, too quiet.

"I wanted a soda that felt like diving into a wave — cold, electric, and impossible to sit still through."

So Han's Splash was born: a business soda for people in motion, charged with sea minerals and built on the simple idea that refreshment should feel alive.
