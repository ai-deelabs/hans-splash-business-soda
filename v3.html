The Han's story

We grew up by the water. The soda just followed.

Founder Han spent every summer chasing the break. The goal was simple: capture that first hit of cold ocean spray and pour it into a can. Years of tinkering later, Splash is that feeling — carbonated.

  • Small batch, big wave. Brewed in coastal micro-runs for peak freshness.
  • Tested on real thirst. Refined by surfers, runners and restless desk crews alike.
  • Always evolving. New seasonal flavors drop with every tide.
